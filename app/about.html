FoodStuffs

is an inventory checklist app that helps grocery stores donate food to soup kitchens

Problem: 10% of Grocery Store non-expired, edible food stock in America is thrown away each year. Thats over 13 billion pounds of food. Most of this food is good for a much longer time frame. Grocery stores have few ways to keep track of their perishable and non-perishable foods. These stores throw out 10% of the food that they have because they do not have an effective tracking system for inventory on the shelf.

We built an inventory system that lets them manage their goods and keep track of the expiration and Sell By dates. So that they can donate or sell unwanted food.

Our system lets stores donate food that they no longer plan to keep, or sell it at a low cost to a local soup kitchen (or food bank) by posting it on our soup kitchen packages page.

This resolves issues with food being thrown out way before the food is indeed bad and give millions of people access to fresh food. It also gives companies a means to easily donate foods to soup kitchens and reap the tax benefits.
