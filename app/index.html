10% of grocery store and deli food stock in America is thrown away each year. That's over 13 billion pounds of food. Let's End Needless Waste!

FoodStuffs an inventory system that lets stores manage their goods and keep track of the expiration and sell by dates of their products. This way stores can donate or sell unsold food to a better cause.
